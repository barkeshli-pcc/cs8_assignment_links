Word Frequency:

Your program uses a large text file (use the  as input.

Tokenizes the file and stores the words on an AVL along with the word's frequency.

Every time you go to insert a word into the tree, if it's already there, increment the frequency.

Then...

You copy the contents of the tree into a Max Heap based on frequency. 

At this point, the word with the highest frequency is at the top.

Now, print the top 100 most frequent words along with their frequency.

 

Both your AVL and Heap are standard structures. You will not make any modifications to these structures for this project.
